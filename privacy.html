Privacy Policy

Our goal is to make an excellent app for you — not to track you. We don’t want to know anything about you unless you contact us yourself. We collect and retain the least information necessary to send you transit information and help you with any occasional problem that might arise.

Key Points:

  • We don't track individual users in any way.
  • We have not, and will not ever sell any data to advertisers or 3rd party companies.
  • If you contact us for support, such as through email, we will delete your information after the resolution of your issue.

Pittsburgh Bus Watch uses the following service providers, which also have access to basic technical information passing through their services and data stored using their resources:

  • Apple (Privacy Policy)
  • Port Authority of Allegheny County’s TrueTime System
